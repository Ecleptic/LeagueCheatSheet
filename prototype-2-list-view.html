Champion Lore

Once honored defenders of Shurima against the Void, Aatrox and his brethren would eventually become an even greater threat to Runeterra, and were defeated only by cunning mortal sorcery. But after centuries of imprisonment, Aatrox was the first to find freedom once more, corrupting and transforming those foolish enough to try and wield the magical weapon that contained his essence. Now, with stolen flesh, he walks Runeterra in a brutal approximation of his previous form, seeking an apocalyptic and long overdue vengeance against the world, as well as a host who might return him to his true nature. The darkin are fallen god-warriors, once revered protectors of ancient Shurima who were corrupted by the Void and later imprisoned within their own weapons. Aatrox was among the greatest of these fallen champions, a noble warrior who fought valiantly against the Void's corruption. However, the endless battles against such cosmic horror eventually drove him and his brethren to madness. They became the very monsters they had sworn to destroy, wielding forbidden blood magic and spreading chaos across the land. When the surviving mortals finally found a way to defeat them, they used cunning magic to trap the darkin within their own weapons, condemning them to an eternity of imprisonment. For centuries, Aatrox lay dormant within his blade, his consciousness fragmented and his power diminished. But the weapon's dark hunger remained, calling out to those who would dare to wield it. When someone finally takes up his sword, Aatrox can possess their body, though he remains only a shadow of his former divine self.
